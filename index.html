This is my web page, where I will slowly be learning how to make beautiful things on the internet. Consider it a sandbox, where increasingly elaborate castles get built and destroyed.

About Me

I'm a budding designer who has spent his college career studying diplomacy. About two years ago I (re)discovered my love for producing art. About a year ago, I realized I would rather work on a cool problem with a small team than work in a dim-lit office within a sprawling bureaucracy.

I'm going to try to post my artwork as well as my design work on this page.

Why am I building this site?

  • I need to learn HTML and CSS and JS and all that jazz
  • I need to put the work that I do up on a website so that the world can see it!
  • I need to get a job, which makes the above two reasons all the more urgent
  • I really enjoy learning new things! I see this as an art project--the limits here are pretty much just my imagination
  • Everyone casually tries to teach themselves HTML. I admit, of this crime I'm a serial offender. This is me finishing what I start
